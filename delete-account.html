Deleting your nudge account

Last updated: 16 June 2026 · Motion Interactive Limited

This page explains how to delete your account on nudge — the location-based app for meeting verified people nearby, operated by Motion Interactive Limited — and what happens to your data when you do.

Delete your account in the app

The fastest way to delete your account is from within the app. It is immediate and permanent — no waiting period.

  1. Open the nudge app and make sure you're signed in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Open the menu (the ⋯ / settings control) and choose Delete account.
  4. Read the warning and confirm. Your account and personal data are deleted straight away.

Request deletion without the app

If you've uninstalled nudge or can't sign in, you can ask us to delete your account by email:

  • Email support@nudgeme.live with the subject "Delete my nudge account".
  • So we can locate and verify your account, include the phone number you registered with.
  • We'll verify that you own the account and complete the deletion within 30 days, then confirm by reply.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the personal information and content associated with it, including:

  • Your profile — name, bio, gender, ethnicity, age and status
  • Your phone number and email address
  • Your profile photos (and the underlying image files)
  • Your location data and map presence
  • Your private messages and nudges (connection requests)
  • Your interests, collectables, coins and personalisation
  • Your identity-verification status and any verification reference we hold
  • Your notifications and push-notification registrations
  • Your nudge Plus subscription record on our systems

What is kept, and why

A small amount of information is retained so that other people's experience and our legal obligations aren't broken:

  • Events you organised remain visible to the people who joined them, but are no longer linked to you — they're shown as organised by "Deleted user", with no personal identifiers attached. Any future events you'd scheduled are cancelled first.
  • Minimal records may be kept where we're required to for legal, security, or fraud- and abuse-prevention purposes, or to resolve a dispute.
  • Purchase records held by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) are controlled by them under their own policies — manage or request deletion of those through your Apple or Google account.

Timing

Deletion from within the app is immediate. Requests made by email are completed within 30 days of us verifying that you own the account. Residual copies in encrypted backups are removed on our normal backup-rotation cycle.
